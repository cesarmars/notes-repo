# How to use percentages

## Using the 1 percent rule
1. What is 23% of 540.

First step find 1 percent of the price 540, you move two decimal places to the left.
- 540. -> 5.40 

Then multiply by the percentage value.
- 5.40 * 23 = 124.3 
  
2. What is 3% of 65.
- 0.65 * 3 =  1.95

3. What is 15% of 132.
- 1.32 * 15 = 19.8
